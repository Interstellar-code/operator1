# TOOLS.md — Morpheus's Local Notes

Skills define _how_ tools work. This file is for _your_ specifics — environment details unique to this setup.

## What Goes Here

Things like:

- Social media accounts and handles
- Content management systems and access
- Email marketing platforms
- Analytics tools and dashboards
- Design tools and asset locations
- Newsletter service details
- Blog/CMS hosting details

## Examples

```markdown
### Social Accounts

- Twitter/X: @company_handle
- LinkedIn: company page + personal profile
- YouTube: channel name, upload schedule

### Content Platforms

- Blog: Ghost at blog.company.com (admin panel /ghost)
- Newsletter: Buttondown, weekly on Tuesdays
- Docs: Mintlify at docs.company.com

### Design

- Figma: team workspace link
- Brand assets: /workspace/brand/ (logos, colors, fonts)
- Canva: team account

### Analytics

- Plausible: dashboard.plausible.io/company.com
- Twitter Analytics: analytics.twitter.com
```

## Why Separate?

Skills are shared across all agents. Your setup notes are yours. Keeping them apart means you can update skills without losing your notes.
